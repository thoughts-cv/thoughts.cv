The importance of remembering students names

14 May, 2026

This blog is about why it’s important to remember students names, the impact it has on teaching, and the relationship we can acquire withe the students/kids. It includes examples from my own life, and my own oberservations of going from never remembering names, to knowing the names of my students after a couple of hours, and remembering them long term.

Example of Gained relation with Student

Come about 1 week later, I see one of the students from that class, exisiting. (Cause what else are you supposed to do waiting for the doors to open at 8:00). I called out to her by name, and the respons I was meet with what was one of schock, and a question “You remembered my name?”. For me the respons was a “Of course”, However, for her this was a random substitute teacher, that came out of nowhere, took the time to learn her name, how it’s spelled, and remember it. it was a substitute teacher that saw her.

Going forward, this child has asked me numerous times when I’d be in their class again, and informed me of her favortisme towards me. I’m assuming it’s because of my willingness to dance “Just dance” on the class projector with them. [write] Round off

Going beyond with Anki

Anki as a tool for remembering names is important, to ensure that we keep ourselves enganged with the name of the person when we are away from them. We can go long stretches of time, where we do no need to remember the name. Therefore we useeanki to remember.
